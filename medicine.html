Svante Pääbo

Svante Pääbo The Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine 2022 Born: 20 April 1955, Stockholm, Sweden Affiliation at the time of the award: Max Planck Institute for Evolutionary Anthropology, Leipzig, Germany, Okinawa Institute of Science and Technology, Okinawa, Japan Prize motivation: “for his discoveries concerning the genomes of extinct hominins and human evolution”
